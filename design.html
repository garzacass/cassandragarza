Roblox Redesign

A fresh take on enhancing user interaction and engagement for an iconic platform. Dove into the full product design process: conducting a UI teardown to identify current areas for improvement, analyzed competitors to gather insights, surveyed diverse users, iterated on designs, and finally created wireframes and prototypes to bring the vision to life.

Frontera Devs

My work with Frontera Devs spans years of leadership and design. From creating feed posts for Discord and Instagram to developing a cohesive branding style, I've made our events instantly recognizable to students. Highlights include designs for our annual hackathon, Frontera Hacks: the website, sponsorship packages, lanyard badges, merch (shirts and stickers), and an extensive suite of social media posts. Every piece reflects a dedication to community-building and creativity.

Handlingo

SheConnect

With the 24 hour restriction that is standard for a hackathon, I crafted this design for SheConnect in just a couple of hours, prioritizing speed without sacrificing usability. This project challenged me to deliver a polished concept quickly, bridging the gap between ideation and coding as quickly as humanly possible.
